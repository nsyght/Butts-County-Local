💬 About The Dumpster Co.

The Dumpster Co. is Jackson's locally owned and operated dumpster rental service, proudly serving Butts County and the surrounding region. Their tagline sums it up perfectly: We Deliver. You Fill. We Haul. No surprise fees, no confusing contracts — just honest pricing and dependable service from a company that's part of the community they serve.

Residential customers rely on The Dumpster Co. for home cleanouts, garage and attic purges, renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, yard waste removal, and estate clear-outs. Book online at mydumpsterco.com, choose your container size and drop-off window, and they take care of the rest.

Commercial dumpster service is a cornerstone of their business. General contractors, roofing companies, demolition crews, property managers, and commercial property owners across Middle Georgia count on The Dumpster Co. for on-time delivery and pickup on active job sites. They understand that in commercial construction, a late dumpster is a scheduling problem that costs money — and their track record speaks for itself.

With a 4.9★ Google rating across 167+ reviews, The Dumpster Co. has earned the trust of Butts County — one project at a time. Call (678) 306-9993 or book online today.
