💬 About Chuck's Tire & Auto Repair

Jackson Auto & Tire has been keeping Butts County vehicles on the road since 2003. Owner-operated with a team of ASE-certified technicians, the shop handles everything from routine oil changes and tire rotations to complex engine diagnostics and transmission work. They carry a wide selection of brand-name tires and offer free flat repair for tires purchased at the shop. Computerized alignment, brake service, AC repair, and state emissions inspection are all available in-house. Jackson Auto & Tire prides itself on straight talk — you'll always get an honest assessment and fair price before any work begins.
