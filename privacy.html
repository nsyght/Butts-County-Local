Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Butts County Local ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website buttscountylocal.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting a business listing or contacting us through our contact form, including your name, email address, business name, phone number, and business description.

We may also collect non-personally identifiable information automatically through standard web server logs and analytics tools, including pages visited, browser type, and referring URLs.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through the Add Listing or Contact forms is used solely to process your request, publish your business listing, and respond to your inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Butts County Local uses minimal cookies for basic site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. Third-party services embedded in our site (such as Google Maps) may set their own cookies subject to their privacy policies.

Google Maps

Our site embeds Google Maps iframes to display business locations. Google's use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain submitted business listing information as long as the listing remains active on the site. Contact form submissions are retained for a reasonable period to facilitate follow-up and are then deleted.

Your Rights

You may request the removal or correction of your business listing at any time by contacting us at hello@buttscountylocal.com.

Children's Privacy

Butts County Local is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at hello@buttscountylocal.com.
