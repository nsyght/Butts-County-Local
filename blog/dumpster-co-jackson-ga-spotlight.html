Business Spotlight: The Dumpster Co. — Jackson's Go-To Roll-Off Rental

March 24, 2026  ·  4 min read
← Back to Blog

When you're knee-deep in a home renovation, estate cleanout, or construction project, the last thing you want to think about is how you're going to get rid of all that debris. That's exactly the problem The Dumpster Co. solves — and they've built a sterling reputation doing it.

Also see: March 2026 Business Spotlight — The Dumpster Co.

Local Roots, Regional Reach

The Dumpster Co. is based right here in Jackson at 10 3rd Street, Suite 201. While they call Butts County home, their service area extends across a wide swath of Middle Georgia, making them accessible to homeowners and contractors across the region.

Residential Services

For homeowners, The Dumpster Co. handles everything from a single-weekend garage cleanout to full estate clear-outs. Spring cleaning, renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, yard waste — whatever the project, they've got the right size container and the scheduling flexibility to work around your timeline. Book online at mydumpsterco.com or call (678) 306-9993.

Commercial Dumpster Service

Commercial work is a major part of The Dumpster Co.'s business. General contractors, roofing companies, demolition crews, property managers, and commercial property owners across Middle Georgia rely on them for consistent, on-time service on active job sites. They understand that in commercial construction, scheduling matters — a late dumpster can hold up an entire crew. Their commercial clients keep coming back because they know The Dumpster Co. will be there when they say they will.

What the Community Says

With a 4.9-star rating on Google across 167 reviews, The Dumpster Co. has earned the trust of Butts County residents and businesses the honest way — by showing up on time, pricing fairly, and making the whole process easy. That kind of rating doesn't happen by accident.

Book Your Dumpster

The easiest way to get started is at mydumpsterco.com where you can check availability and book online. Or call them directly at (678) 306-9993. Tell them Butts County Local sent you.
