March 2026 Spotlight

The Dumpster Co.

🏗️ Construction & Waste · Jackson, GA 30233

Some businesses fill a gap in the market. The Dumpster Co. fills a dumpster — and they do it better than anyone else in Butts County. The tagline says it all: We Deliver. You Fill. We Haul. Simple, dependable, and exactly what every homeowner and contractor needs.

Founded and based right here in Jackson at 10 3rd Street, Suite 201, The Dumpster Co. has become the go-to roll-off dumpster rental service across Butts County and the surrounding region. No confusing pricing, no runaround when you call. You book a dumpster, they drop it where you need it, you fill it on your schedule, and they haul it away when you're done.

Residential services cover everything homeowners need: spring cleanouts, garage and attic purges, renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, yard waste, and estate clear-outs. Book online in minutes at mydumpsterco.com, pick your size and drop-off window, and they handle the rest.

Commercial dumpster service is a major part of what The Dumpster Co. does. General contractors, roofing crews, demolition teams, property managers, and commercial property owners across Middle Georgia rely on them for consistent, on-time delivery and pickup on active job sites. They understand that in commercial construction a late dumpster isn't just an inconvenience — it's a scheduling problem that costs real money. The Dumpster Co. gets that, and their commercial clients keep coming back because of it.

With a 4.9★ rating on Google backed by over 167 reviews, the community has spoken. Whether you've got a weekend home project or an active commercial job site, The Dumpster Co. is the call to make first.
